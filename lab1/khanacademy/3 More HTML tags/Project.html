Recipe #1: Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Eggs 3 large
Parmesan cheese 50g
Pancetta 150g
Salt & pepper To taste

Step 1: Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.

Step 2: Whisk eggs and grated Parmesan in a bowl.

Step 3: Fry pancetta until crispy, then mix with drained spaghetti.

Step 4: Remove from heat and quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture.

Step 5: Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.

Source: Simply Recipes

Recipe #2: Chicken Curry

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken breast 500g
Onion 1 large
Garlic cloves 3
Ginger 1 tbsp
Curry powder 2 tbsp
Coconut milk 400ml
Vegetable oil 2 tbsp

Step 1: Heat oil in a pan and sauté chopped onion, garlic, and ginger.

Step 2: Add curry powder and stir until aromatic.

Step 3: Add chicken and cook until browned.

Step 4: Pour in coconut milk, simmer for 20 minutes until thickened.

Step 5: Serve with rice or flatbread.

Source: BBC Good Food

Recipe #3: Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 12 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 200g
Butter 100g
Sugar 150g
Egg 1 large
Vanilla extract 1 tsp
Chocolate chips 150g

Step 1: Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).

Step 2: Cream butter and sugar, then mix in egg and vanilla.

Step 3: Fold in flour and chocolate chips.

Step 4: Shape dough into balls, place on a baking sheet.

Step 5: Bake for 12–15 minutes. Cool and enjoy!

Source: All Recipes
